About Me

I am a future graduate in Computer Science, graduating on December 2020. I also have a graduation in Business Administration, back from when I lived in Brazil. After having experiences working with business, I decided to follow my dream and change careers. That decision brought me to Canada, where I started my studies at the University of Manitoba.

Having a first degree in Business Administration and a vast work experience has significantly influenced my interpersonal skills, my ability to communicate, my capacity for empathy, and my teamwork performance.

As a co-op student, I have been obtaining experience to build a solid base to my career. During my three co-op terms, I was able to apply the knowledge from the University right into the workplace. This journey increased my passion for developing web applications with beautiful, engaging UIs. Being a co-op student also allowed me to refine my ability to pick up new software, frameworks, and APIs.
